About TEI Studio

A boutique interior design studio built on the belief that beautiful spaces change how we feel and live.

Our Story

Rooted in Malaysian living.

TEI Studio was founded with a simple conviction: that interior design should be deeply personal. We draw on Malaysia's rich culture of craft, material, and light to create spaces that feel rooted and real — never generic, never imposed.

Our Approach

We listen before we design.

Every project begins with conversation. We take time to understand how you move through a space, what you love, and what you need — then translate that into a design language that is uniquely yours. The result is always thoughtful, always functional, always beautiful.

What We Believe

Quiet confidence over trend.

We design for longevity. That means choosing materials that age well, palettes that endure, and layouts that grow with you. We resist the impulse to follow fashion, preferring instead to follow the logic of each space and the life that will be lived in it.
